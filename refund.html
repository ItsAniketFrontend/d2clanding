Payment Issues and Resolution

We use PhonePe as our official payment gateway to securely process transactions. In rare cases, your payment may be declined or fail to process. Please note, we do not receive detailed reasons for declined transactions. If this occurs, we advise the following steps:

Contact Your Bank or Card Issuer Directly – especially if:
  • You've used the same payment method successfully in the past.
  • The payment failed despite having sufficient funds or credit.
  • Your bank has flagged the transaction due to security concerns or unusual activity.
Possible Issues & Suggested Actions:
  • Security Flags: Banks may block first-time or high-value purchases. Contact your bank to authorize the transaction.
  • Daily Limits: Most banks set daily transaction limits. Ask your bank if this is restricting your payment and request a temporary increase if needed.
  • Authorization Holds: For failed transactions, your bank may still reserve funds. These holds typically clear within 7–10 business days. Request your bank to release any blocked or held amount, if applicable.
  • Use an Alternate Payment Method: If you are unable to resolve the issue promptly, we recommend trying another card or UPI account for successful completion of your order.
